Know Akilah & Davis Family

Davis college Akilah is a campus that deals with educating east Africa's future leaders.Every element of Davis College has been intentionally designed to create a radically different educational experience, replacing traditional education methods like large group lectures and rote memorization with customized resources and activities to address the unique needs of each student, including group projects, tailored academic support, career coaching, and community service projects.
